Progressive Web App Development

Build your PWA with us.

Step by Step

PWAs are web applications that leverage service workers, manifests, and other web-platform characteristics along with progressive enhancement to provide users with an experience comparable to that of native apps.PWAs provide a number of advantages to users

  • installable
  • progressively enhanced
  • responsively designed
  • re-engageable
  • linkable
  • instant updates
Planning

A team of full stack developers at Nanostuffs analyses your requirements and specifications, takes them into account, and creates a rough plan for your approval.

Designing

During the design process, everything from an effective framework to an intuitive interface is drafted, looked at, assessed, and approved.

Defining

We carry out background checks, get your approval on features, establish deadlines, conduct feasibility tests, and complete the design.

Building

Our programmers work very hard to make sure your software is as efficient as possible, using the most recent technology such as React Js, Angular Js, Node, and Express to develop the application.

Testing

We use a variety of tests to examine the software's functionality and security. Test-driven development is what we do.

Deployment & Maintenance

The programme is released to the environment of your choice after receiving clearance. You now own all rights to the customised solution. We work hard to make all maintenance straight forward enough for your staff to handle, and we provide ongoing maintenance for more intricate and/or extensive programmes.
